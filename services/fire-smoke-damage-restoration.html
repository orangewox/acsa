FIRE & SMOKE DAMAGE REstoration

The damage caused by a fire can bring devastation to your home or business. We respond quickly and effectively to mitigate the damage to your property. Timing is critical when it comes to the restoration of a property that has experienced fire and smoke damage. Even after a fire is fully extinguished, the damage is far from over. We come in immediately to begin the process of saving and restoring certain materials. Due to the array of chemical reactions created by synthetic materials, time only allows as little as days to save them.

Commercial Fire & Smoke Damage Restoration

-

Damage from Fire & Smoke poses a serious threat to your business operations. Our goal is to quickly and efficiently restore your building back to pre-loss condition. The residue left behind after a fire should be handled by trained professionals, as it is hazardous and can cause health problems to your staff. We offer a comprehensive Commercial Fire & Smoke Damage Restoration Service inclusive of the following:

Commercial Fire & Smoke Damage Restoration Services

Commercial Fire & Smoke Damage Cleanup
  • Collecting and removing contents destroyed by fire & smoke damage
  • Containing affected areas to prevent smoke damage to other areas of your building even after the fire is out, such as walls, flooring, furnishings, equipment, and others
  • Perform cleaning, eliminating odors, and deodorizing surfaces to pre-loss condition
Structural Inspection & Restoration
  • Inspection of building structures to address damage to surfaces, materials, and physical structure
  • Even even after a fire is put out, these areas must be treated for smoke odors and preventing leakage of odors back into other areas of the building
  • In spect and remove any charred contents that may potentially seal in smoke (insulation, wood, etc.)
Remediating Water Damage (If present)
  • Remediate water exposure from sprinkler systems
  • Mitigation of residual water from  fire department efforts to extinguish the fire
  • Apply specialized fans and dehumidifiers to scrub the air of smells and dry out the structure from water exposure
